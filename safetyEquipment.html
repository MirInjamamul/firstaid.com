How to customize a first aid kit

We're excited to share a new way to create a custom first aid kit! MFASCO Health & Safety, the leader in first aid kits has created "Make A Kit", the Internet's first and only online tool that allows you to create your own first aid kit. Pick the container and supplies in a few easy steps.

We've developed 3 new ways to quickly make your own custom first aid kit using Make A Kit! Anytime you see MAK (the "Make A Kit" character), you will have the opportunity customize that product. You can also make or customize other kits including an emergency response kit, bleeding control kit, burn kit, or hygiene kit with Make A Kit. Take a look below and see how easy it is to customize an existing kit.

Customize an existing first aid kit

We have a list of first aid kits you can customize with the Make A Kit tool. Simply select the kit from the list and you will be directed to the product page. Click on MAK and the words "Customize This Kit" and you will be taken to Make A Kit with this kit already loaded. Mouse over the puzzle piece and the list will appear. Edit quantities or remove items. If you want to add items, look at the category list to the left. Choose a category and then select new items to add to your kit. Continue until you are finished. Pay attention to the kit capacity percentage to make sure you don't over fill your kit. When ready, click the green button that says "Finish Adding Products", answer a few questions and your done.

Start with your favorite empty container

from the empty container product page, Click on MAK and the words "Customize This Kit" and you will be taken to Make A Kit with this empty kit already included in your selection. Answer a few questions and begin filling your container with supplies.

Use the Make A Kit tool

Make A Kit is the Internet's first and only online tool that allows you to create your own custom first aid kit from scratch. Answer a few questions, select your container & products from the easy to use tool and we will make your custom first aid kit.
